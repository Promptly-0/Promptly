# Our Business Model

The Promptly team believes that the software industry is tired of expensive monthly subscriptions. We use an outcome-based revenue model.

"We Only Win When You Win."

## 1. Performance Fee
We take a **15% fee** only on the net profits generated by the AI during a trading session. If your agent executes trades but doesn't make any money, you are not charged a performance fee.

## 2. Platform Fee
A tiny flat fee of **$0.05** is applied each time an autonomous and secure trading session is opened via the Guardrail Engine.

## 3. Data Routing Margin
We add a microscopic surcharge (a fraction of a cent) whenever the AI uses the Promptly network router to purchase outside market data.
