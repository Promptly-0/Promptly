# Getting Started

Promptly acts as the financial nervous system for your AI, built on the Tempo network (backed by Stripe). 

Instead of giving AI the keys to your entire vault, Promptly uses a system similar to a temporary, prepaid debit card.

## 1. Initializing Sessions
You grant your AI agent a specific "Session Budget" (e.g., "$500 for the next 12 hours"). 
Your AI can trade as fast as it wants within that window, but it physically cannot spend a penny more than authorized.

## 2. Pay-As-You-Go Intelligence
Allow your AI to autonomously buy the exact data it needs, exactly when it needs it. 
If the AI detects a market shift and needs to read a premium financial report, it can instantly pay a fraction of a cent for that single piece of data—no human credit card required.
