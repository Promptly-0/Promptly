# Technical Architecture

Promptly is designed as a deep financial settlement layer specifically engineered for Artificial Intelligence systems.

## The Problem: AI is Financially Blind
Today, giving an AI the ability to trade introduces massive friction points:
1. **The Subscription Bottleneck**: Algorithms cannot spontaneously subscribe to critical market data points.
2. **The Speed Limit**: Traditional banking limits sub-second automation.
3. **The Trust Issue**: Giving untethered access to a human user's wallet could drain accounts instantly due to hallucination risks.

## Digital Dollar Native (Tempo L1)
To avoid the headaches and speedbumps of traditional blockchains, Promptly natively settles all fees in digital dollars (USDC) over the **Tempo Network**.
Developed in partnership with Stripe and Paradigm, this architecture guarantees institutional-grade speed and absolute transaction finality without gas-volatility nightmares.
