# Promptly: The Financial Operating System for AI

Welcome to the Promptly Developer Documentation!

We are building the first autonomous trading terminal that gives AI agents the ability to securely manage capital, purchase real-time market data, and execute trades at lightning speed—all while keeping the human user in total control.

## Overview

The internet is shifting from a web of human browsing to an **Agentic Web**. However, while AI has become incredibly smart, it remains *financially paralyzed*.

If you want an AI to trade for you today, you face three roadblocks:
1. **The Subscription Bottleneck**: AI can't just buy market data ad-hoc.
2. **The Speed Limit**: Manual transaction signing defeats the purpose.
3. **The Trust Issue**: Giving AI unrestricted access to your wallet is incredibly dangerous.

**Promptly solves this.** Built on the Tempo network and backed by Stripe, we provide the financial nervous system for your AI, natively executing in digital dollars.
